About the Sci-Tech Council

The Sci-Tech Council of IIT Bhilai serves as the apex body to oversee and coordinate activities of all technical clubs. It aims to enhance student engagement in technology-driven projects, competitions, and knowledge-sharing platforms.

"To unify the technical potential of students across diverse domains, fostering a culture of innovation and teamwork."
- Sci-Tech Council Mission Statement

Our Philosophy

Inclusivity, collaboration, and innovation guide every initiative we undertake. The Council encourages all students to participate, learn, and lead.
