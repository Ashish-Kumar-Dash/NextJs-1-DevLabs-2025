ABOUT US

Ingenuity as the name suggests is a club for the problem solvers! The prime motive of the club is to promote and enrich the coding culture in IIT Bhilai. The club provides a constructive and budding environment to the students who share a passion for problem solving and competitive programming. The club organises events and contests along with workshops and seminars on competitive programming with the support of a dedicated team of core members.

It's a great place where we all come together to learn programming and build cool projects. We often have fun solving coding challenges, and everyone is really helpful. We also organize workshops and small competitions, which make learning more exciting.

We also do a lot of CP—that's short for Competitive Programming. It’s all about solving tricky coding problems within a time limit, kind of like a mental workout. In the club, we practice on platforms like Codeforces and LeetCode, and we often discuss different approaches after solving a problem. It really sharpens your thinking and helps with placements too.
