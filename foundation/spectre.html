Security Domains

security domains are broadly categorized into:

  • Network Security

    • It involves various components of a network,each of which can present potential points of vulnerability that attackers may exploit.

  • Cryptography

    • A technique of securing information by transforming it into an unreadable format to enhance the confidentiality,authenticity of data.

  • Ethical Hacking

    • A way of intentionally hacking our own systems/softwares to detect and fix crucial vulnerabilities in an ethical manner.
