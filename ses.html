Society Overview

Welcome to SES — where we explore the wonders of the cosmos through astronomy, astrophysics, and aeromodelling. Our motto:

"From Proximity to Infinity"

Space Exploration Society (SES)

We, at Space Exploration Society, IIT Bhilai, are present to promote awareness and work forward in the fields of astronomy, aeromodelling and astrophysics

Our Divisions

Astronomy

Exploring the night sky, stargazing, and astrophotography.

Astrophysics

Understanding the physics of celestial bodies and cosmic phenomena.

Aeromodelling

Building and flying model rockets and drones with practical workshops.
